🐪 骆驼骑行陷阱Camel Ride Trap

上骆驼前说"one dollar"甚至免费，下来变脸要100美元。有人直接把你抱上去"全自动体验"。不骑骆驼、骆驼入镜了也要收摄影费，不给就来三个大汉删你照片。 "Free ride" on the way up, $100 to dismount. Some forcibly lift you onto the camel. Even if a camel just appears in your photo background, they demand a "photography fee." Best advice: don't engage at all.

👔 假工作人员Fake Officials

穿制服戴胸牌，热情带你参观，结束后伸手要1–100美元。保安、景区工作人员都有可能是骗子，甚至真正的工作人员也会伸手要小费。找不到地方只问穿制服的警察。 People in unofficial uniforms guide you through a site, then demand large tips. Even real staff sometimes demand tips. If lost, only ask uniformed police — never follow a "helpful stranger."

🎓 免费向导骗局"Free Guide" Scam

"我是大学教授/学生，练习中文，免费带你游览。"游览结束立刻要钱。天下没有免费向导，找导游只在景点入口售票处找有旅游部徽章的正规人员。 "I'm a history professor practicing my English." After the tour, they demand payment. There is no such thing as a free tour. Only hire guides with an official Ministry of Tourism badge at ticket counters.

💸 "One Dollar"强卖The "One Dollar" Hustle

卖家说one dollar，接着往你手里塞更多东西索要高价。小孩也用这招乞讨，给了一个就会有一群追你跑800米。不要接任何陌生人递来的东西。 A vendor quotes "one dollar" then shoves more items into your hands and demands far more. Give to one child and a crowd will chase you. Never accept anything handed to you unsolicited.

清真寺Mosques

🕌 清真寺"向导"Mosque Guide Scam

有人恰好和你一起进清真寺，主动讲解，结束后说"那是一次参观，请付费"，要价高达100埃镑。大多数清真寺免费进入，有票亭才是真实收费的。 Someone walks in beside you and starts explaining the mosque. At the end they say "that was a paid tour." Most mosques are free. If there's a fee, there's a clear ticket booth — don't let anyone walk with you uninvited.

兑换Currency

🏧 机场不要多换钱Don't Exchange at the Airport

开罗机场汇率低，落地只换必要数量。到市中心 Exchange Store 再换，汇率更好。美元在旅游区普遍接受，也可直接使用。 Airport exchange rates are poor. Only change what you need to get to the city, then use licensed Exchange stores downtown for significantly better rates. USD is accepted at most tourist sites.

找零Change

🪙 故意不找零The "No Change" Trick

部分商贩以"没零钱"为由吞零头。出门随身带好小额零钱，尽量支付精确金额。小额零钱也是给小费的必需品。 Vendors frequently claim they have no change for large bills. Pay the exact amount when possible. Carry a mix of 5, 10, 20 EGP notes for small purchases and tips.

🛍️ 集市砍价从10%开始Start at 10% of Ask Price

集市购物从报价的10–20%开始砍，表现出可买可不买的态度，转身就走是最强杀招——他们会叫你回来。墨镜很管用，不对眼就不被纠缠。 Counter-offer at 10–20% of the asking price. Show complete indifference — turn and walk away. They almost always call you back at a much lower price. This works because they need the sale more than you do.

吃饭用右手，不用左手（左手被认为不洁）。送礼和接礼用双手或右手。不要在街头公开饮酒（只在持牌餐厅和酒店）。Eat with your right hand — the left is considered unclean in Islamic tradition. Use both hands or your right hand to give and receive items. Don't drink alcohol on the street; only at licensed bars or hotel venues.

⏰ 时差与作息Time Zone & Rhythm

阿斯旺 ↔ 卢克索Aswan ↔ Luxor
